Angela's Recipe for the best Cinamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teasspon yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup darl brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon ground cinammon
  • 1/4 cup butter
